# Mountain Goats - Game Rules

Based on research from BGA and game description.

## Objective
Be the first to score 12 points by getting your goats to mountain peaks and holding them.

## Setup
- 6 mountains numbered 5-10
- Each mountain has 3 spaces: bottom (5), middle (6), top (7/8/9/10 depending on mountain)
- Each player starts with 12 goats in their hand (2 per die value: 5,6,7,8,9,10)
- Dice pool starts full (10×5, 9×6, 8×7, 7×8, 6×9, 5×10)

## Turn Structure

1. **Select a die** from the available pool
2. **Choose a goat** to move:
   - Either place a new goat from your hand onto any mountain's bottom space (5)
   - Or move an existing goat that's already on the board
3. **Move the goat** to a space matching the die number
   - Can move to same mountain or different mountain
   - Must move to exact space matching die value (5→6, 6→7, etc.)
4. **Kicking**: If destination space is occupied, kick that goat off
   - Kicked goat returns to owner's hand
   - Can only have 1 goat per space
5. **Peak scoring**: If you have goat(s) on top spaces at end of your turn, score points
   - 1 point per goat on a peak (top space of any mountain)
6. **Remove die** from pool (used dice don't return)

## Movement Rules

- Goats can move from any space to any space with matching die value
- Can cross mountains (mountain 5 space 6 → mountain 8 space 7 if using die 7)
- Only 1 goat per space - occupant gets kicked
- Can't stay on same space
- New goats from hand always enter at bottom (space 5)

## Winning

First player to 12 points wins immediately.

## Strategy Notes

- Top spaces (peaks) = scoring
- Kicking opponents off peaks stops their scoring
- Dice pool depletes - higher numbers run out faster
- Limited goats (12 total) - manage placement carefully
